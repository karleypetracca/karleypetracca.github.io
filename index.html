So I'm finally taking the plunge and following my passion into web development.

Why web development?

When I think back on the years, its easy to see how the signs were there all along pointing to web development:

  • My teenage days were spent using HTML to build the perfect MySpace page.
  • Digital art has been a hobby since childhood, forcing me to learn about creative design as well as positioning / placement / margins / etc for online display.
  • I was the "oddball" in school (and later work!) who enjoyed creating complicated formulas in excel and other similar programs.
  • Data analysis was something I gravitated to in my career and greatly enjoyed learning Tableau, Python, R and SQL to stay competitive.
